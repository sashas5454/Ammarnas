About the book

"Ammarnäsbygdens historia 1803-2008 : Nedtecknad av nybyggarnas ättlingar" is the full title of a compilation of accounts and events detailing the history of a small village located in the Västerbotten county of northern Sweden. The information was gathered by a local study group that was formed in 2005 through the initiative of the Ammarnäs local history society. Their work was based on previous material collected and documented by a similar study group in the 1970's.

Bibliographic information:

Title:Ammarnäsbygdens historia : Nedtecknad av nybyggarnas ättlingar
Publishing year: 2008
Publisher: Ammarnäs hembygdsförening
Total number of pages: 224 p.
Language: Swedish
Illustrations: Black and white photographs and maps

About the digitization project

The purpose of preserving cultural and intellectual resources is to make their use possible at some unknown future time. By laying the foundation of making this book available online we hope that it will find new readers, perhaps among the descendants of the families who lived in Ammarnäs or among researchers interested in the local history of the area. An important part of the project is not only to make the book available but also to preserve it by encoding its content, and thereby make it even more useful in terms of information retrieval and research.

The current project focuses on digitizing the first chapter of "Ammarnäsbygdens historia 1803-2008", with the hope to continue the process for the remainder of the book.

Special thanks to Sylvia Berg for providing the copyright for the book.

This website is a part of the Digitisation of Cultural Heritage Material course at the University of Borås.
