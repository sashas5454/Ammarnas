About this project:

"Ammarnäsbygdens historia 1803-2008" is a compilation of accounts and events detailing the history of a small village located in the Västerbotten county of northern Sweden. The information was gathered by a local study group that was formed in 2005 through the initiative of the Ammarnäs local history society. Their work was based on previous material collected and documented by a similar study group in the 1970's.

This website is a part of the Digitisation of Cultural Heritage Material course at the University of Borås.

The current project focuses on digitizing the first chapter of "Ammarnäsbygdens historia 1803-2008", with the hope to continue the digitization process for the remainder of the book.
